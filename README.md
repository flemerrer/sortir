# SORTIR

## About the app

SORTIR is a web app to make plans with people from your local student community. You can discover what awesome activities are happening around you and join them in a couple seconds. 

Language study groups, charity gatherings, afterwork drinks, sports and more. We want to make it easy for you to organize your meetups. Have an idea ? Create a post on SORTIR and start inviting people right now !
